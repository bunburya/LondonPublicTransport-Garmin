LPTG (London Public Transport for Garmin) is a simple widget that shows you current line status and predicted arrivals and departures for certain Transport for London (TfL) stops and routes.

The app is powered by TfL Open Data (but has not been developed by and is not affiliated with TfL).

More information, including configuration and usage instructions, can be found at https://github.com/bunburya/LPTG
